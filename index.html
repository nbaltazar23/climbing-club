San Diego Climbing Club

About Us

San Diego Climbing Club bring together rock climbing enthusiasts of all experience levels through meetups at local and destination outdoor climbing venues, indoor rock climbing gyms, and social gatherings such as movie nights and BBQs. Those new to rock climbing can benefit through beginner friendly meetups geared toward introducing the sport with some basic and beneficial information. Experienced climbers will easily find potential climbing partners through any of the gatherings this meetup has to offer. Members span the full gamut of climbing styles, from indoor gym climbing, to outdoor sport and multipitch traditional climbing, and even alpine/ice and mountaineering. San Diego Climbing Club promotes safe rock climbing through informational meetups that expose members to new safety concepts and techniques. Our members and organizers are constantly learning from others and happily sharing their knowledge. And most of all, we get out and climb!

Events

Most of our members generally work 9-5pm jobs so we usually meet at climbing gyms on the weekday nights. On weekends we will meet up at desginated locations for outdoor climbing sessions followed by a social hour at a local bar/brewery. If you have a flexible work schedule, make sure to exchange your contact info with other members for impromptu meetup sessions.

Our Members

Member spotlight

We like to show our new members some appreciation and get a brief confessional of their experience so far with our group.

Daisy Doze

I used to be scared of heights but rock climbing has always drawn my attention. I went climbing at a gym once and saw a group of people having a good time climbing and it turned out to be this climbing group. I wanted to be a part of this community so I signed up. The support I've received when starting out and conquering my fears is unlike anything I've felt before. Not one regret since joining.

Ted Thorpador

I was new in town but am considered to be an avid climber. When I got to San Diego, I did not climb much because I did not have anyone to climb with. I went to a gym and saw a flyer about this group to come to a climbing meetup. I met a lot of cool people here and climbers of my skill level too! Since then, I've been going to every event thrown and haven't regretted it since.
